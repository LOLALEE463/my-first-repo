2003

In 2003, Rong Baisheng Architectural Structure Design Firm (General Partnership) (hereinafter referred to as RBS) was officially established. Over these years, we have emerged from a new company to gradually become a leading enterprise in the industry, the largest single structural discipline professional Grade A design firm in China.

In past twenty years, we have collaborated with clients to create many magnificent architectural masterpieces - more than one thousand projects RBS conducted structural design and consultancy, well known for super high-rise, long span, and complex structures. Large commercial complexes, luxury residential buildings, long span stadium, office, and hotel projects spread all over the country. Either it is a super tall skyscraper or an elegant leisurely courtyard architecture, RBS can compliment the power and beauty of architecture with economically reasonable structural system.

We continue to move forward, challenging traditional industry rules and boundaries, pursuing innovation and breakthrough. RBS team has made great achievements in many technical fields, such as advanced analysis, seismic (vibration), industrialization, geotechnical engineering, high-end strengthening, project management timber structure, TOD development, wind engineering. Many independently developed systems and products have been successfully applied to practical projects. RBS has contributed to industry excellence with unique technological strength and innovation.

Twenty years journey of growth has been full of challenges and hardworking, RBS has always adhered to the pursuit of excellence. In future, we will continue as always to advance in structural engineering, and extend the horizon with professional and innovative wings.
